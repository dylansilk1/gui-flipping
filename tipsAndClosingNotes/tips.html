Closing notes

Lastly are just some general tips that I think have helped me with flipping items. An extremely useful tool that you can use is google sheets or excel. It will take some time to get yourself an operating sheet, but once you have it, it’s an incredibly useful tool to keep track of your expenses. You can track items that you find, their prices, how much it costs to shop, gas prices, and many other things that may otherwise prove difficult to track. Another useful tool is some way of keeping data on when and where you’re finding items, as finding inventory can be hard. This tool will help you narrow down when you should be hitting certain areas, such as thrift stores. All in all, that’s a very rough guide on how you can get started with flipping, and hopefully make it into a profitable little business.
